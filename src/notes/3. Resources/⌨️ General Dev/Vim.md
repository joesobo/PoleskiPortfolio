## Modes
- `Normal` - Navigate default, Press `Esc` or `CTRL + C` to navigate to
- `Insert` - Typing, Press `i` to navigate to
- `Visual` - Selecting, Press `v` to navigate to

## Important Key Combos
- `hjkl` - Navigate
- `y` - Copy
- `p` - Paste
- `/{term}` - Search
